Benyamin Bashari is currently an M.Sc. student at the University of Calgary, he studied his Bachelor degree in Computer Engineering at the Ferdowsi University of Mashhad. He is a Gold medalist of the National Scientific Olympiad in Iran (2018), and won a Gold medal in ACM Programming Contest Rocky Mountain Regional (2019), and also two Silver medals in ACM Programming Contest West Asia Tehran Regional (2016-2017). His research interests are Distributed Algorithm, Randomized Algorithm.
